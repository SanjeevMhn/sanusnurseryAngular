About Us

At Sanu's Nursery, we are passionate about plants and nature. What started as a response to the pandemic has blossomed into a thriving plant nursery. Our journey began when we recognized the need for greenery and natural beauty in people's lives during these challenging times. Inspired by the healing power of plants and the desire to create a serene environment, we embarked on a mission to provide a wide range of plants to our community.

As a locally-owned and operated plant nursery, we are deeply committed to our community and the environment. We strive to promote sustainable practices by using eco-friendly materials, offering organic options, and advocating for responsible gardening. We also actively participate in community initiatives, collaborating with local organizations to spread awareness about the benefits of plants and fostering a love for nature among people of all ages.
